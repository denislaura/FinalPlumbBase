Company Overview

Plumb Base Uganda Ltd is a leading engineering company specialising in electro-mechanical works, water and sewerage systems, power and energy solutions, and industrial infrastructure projects for both public and private sector clients.

Plumb Base Uganda Ltd is a multidisciplinary engineering company providing innovative and reliable electro-mechanical solutions across Uganda and the region. Through our commitment to quality, professionalism, and technical excellence, we have successfully delivered projects for government institutions, international organisations, and private sector clients.
