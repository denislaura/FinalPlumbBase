About Our Company

Plumb Base Uganda Ltd was established to provide innovative engineering solutions that support sustainable development across Uganda and the wider region. Over the years, the company has grown into a trusted engineering partner delivering complex electro-mechanical, water, sanitation, energy and infrastructure projects.

Our success is built on technical expertise, professionalism, integrity and a commitment to delivering projects that exceed client expectations.
